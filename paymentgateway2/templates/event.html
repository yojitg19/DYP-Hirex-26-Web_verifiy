Event Timeline

1

Register & Pay

Attendee payment is captured and held in escrow until the event outcome is confirmed.

2

48h Warning

Organizer receives a reminder before the event. Cancellation here triggers a full refund.

3

QR Check-in

Attendees scan a QR code at the venue to confirm attendance and update the attendance rate.

4

Post-Event Feedback

Attendees submit ratings and complaints during the 48–72h review window.

5

Payout Decision

Escrow is released, held, or frozen based on rating and complaint metrics.
