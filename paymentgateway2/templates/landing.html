System Overview

The Escrow & Payout system manages payment flows between attendees, organizers, and the platform:

Attendee Protection

Payments are held in escrow until event completion and quality verification. Full refunds issued if event is cancelled.

Organizer Incentives

Payouts released upon meeting quality thresholds: minimum rating (3.5+) and complaint rate cap (≤10%).

Platform Operations

5% processing fee on successful payouts. Objective decision rules ensure fairness and transparency across all transactions.
