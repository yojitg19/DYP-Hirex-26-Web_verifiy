Available Scenarios

Each scenario demonstrates different payment outcomes and decision paths:

SUCCESS

Successful Event Completion

Event concludes with high attendance, positive feedback, and low complaints. Payout is processed and released to organizer.

Expected Rating: 4.2+ Expected Complaints: <5% Outcome: Full Payout
CANCELLATION

Event Cancellation & Refund

Event is cancelled within 48 hours of scheduled start time. All attendee funds are immediately refunded without platform fees.

Cancellation Window: <48h Refund Rate: 100% Outcome: Full Refund
INVESTIGATION

Escrow Frozen - Quality Issues

Event occurs but complaint threshold is exceeded (>10% of attendees). Escrow is frozen pending administrative investigation and resolution.

Complaint Rate: >10% Expected Rating: <3.5 Outcome: Hold & Review
HOLD

Low Attendance & Extended Hold

Event occurs but check-in rate is critically low (<10%). System flags for admin review due to suspicious activity or lack of interest.

Check-in Rate: <10% Registered vs Attended: High Variance Outcome: Held Pending Review
